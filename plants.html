find your perfect plant

It's a common misconception that all plants are really hard to take care of. But if you choose a plant that suits your space and learn how often to water it, your plant will be thriving in no time!

Scroll down to read about the various options, and then use the buttons below to filter the selection of plants based on what best suits you and your space.

sun-lovers

are best suited to sunny windows that receive direct sunlight for at least 6 hours every day.

shade-lovers

are best suited to windows that don't get any direct sunglight, or away from the windows in rooms that get direct light.

humidity-lovers

are best suited to rooms with higher humidity such as bathrooms or conservatories, or near a humidifier.

easy-care

are best suited to first-time plant parents, these are very hardy plants which can survive in a variety of locations with minimal attention.
